## PROSPECT RESEARCH METHODOLOGY

### Target Profile:
- **Rate:** £25+/hour (premium tutors)
- **Experience:** 2+ years (established, higher commission rates)  
- **Volume:** 30+ lessons/month (£200+ commission loss)
- **Commission Tier:** 25-33% (maximum pain point)
- **Geographic:** UK/EU/US (primary market fit)

### Commission Loss Calculation:
```
Monthly Revenue × Commission Rate = Monthly Loss
Monthly Loss × 12 = Annual Loss
```

Example: £2,000/month tutor at 25% = £500/month = £6,000/year loss
