### Automation 2: Calendar Sync Across Platforms

**Time Saved:** 1+ hour/week

**What It Does:** Keeps your availability synchronized across all platforms and personal calendarsâ€”no double-bookings, no manual updating.

**Why It Matters:**
- Prevents double-booking disasters
- One source of truth for availability
- Personal life and work life stay separated
- Changes propagate automatically

**What to Sync:**
- Google Calendar (primary)
- Booking tool (Calendly, TutorLingua, etc.)
- Preply/iTalki calendars (if applicable)
- Personal calendar (block personal time)

**How to Set Up:**

**Option 1: Native Calendar Sync (Easiest)**
Most booking tools sync directly with Google Calendar:
1. Connect Google Calendar in booking tool settings
2. Enable two-way sync
3. All bookings appear in Google Calendar automatically
4. Blocked time in Google appears as unavailable for booking

**Option 2: Zapier Integration (More Control)**
1. Create Zap: "When new booking in [Tool] â†’ Create Google Calendar event"
2. Create reverse Zap for cancellations
3. Add additional actions (Slack notification, email to self, etc.)

**Option 3: Cal.com (Open Source Alternative)**
1. Free, open-source booking
2. Native calendar sync
3. Connect multiple calendars
4. Good for privacy-conscious tutors

**Pro Tip:** Block personal time in your primary calendar FIRST, then it syncs to booking tools as "unavailable." Your dentist appointment automatically blocks lesson bookings.

**Setup Difficulty:** Easy
